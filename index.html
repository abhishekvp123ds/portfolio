About Me.

With over a decade of experience in data analytics and business intelligence, I specialize in transforming raw data into actionable insights that drive performance and growth. I currently work as a Senior Data Specialist at Randstad Offshore Services, where I lead reporting and dashboarding initiatives in HR and sales analytics. My journey began at Accenture, where I focused on automation and supply chain optimization, building robust reporting tools that supported data-driven decisions.

As a data analyst, I follow the end-to-end lifecycle of BI project delivery—starting from understanding stakeholder requirements, to designing data models, creating ETL pipelines, building dashboards, and delivering insights. I apply Agile methodologies to structure my workflow using short sprints, iterative delivery, and continuous feedback. During each sprint cycle, I prioritize features using Scrum ceremonies such as daily stand-ups, sprint planning, backlog grooming, and retrospectives.

My technical expertise includes Power BI for dynamic dashboards and DAX-based KPIs, SQL for data wrangling and performance-optimized queries, and Excel for advanced formula logic, VBA automation, and ad-hoc reporting. I also bring in storytelling and visual design skills to ensure data isn’t just analyzed—it’s understood.

Whether you're a startup founder looking for a freelance analyst or a company in need of full-time BI expertise, I offer reliability, clarity, and a deep understanding of what it takes to turn data into a strategic asset. Let's collaborate to uncover the insights you need to grow.
