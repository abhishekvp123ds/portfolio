About Me

I am Abhishek Vasudevan, a consulting data analyst and business intelligence specialist with 10 years of experience, passionate about helping organizations make effective use of their data.

From conceptualizing what to measure and ensuring accurate data collection, to data preparation, analysis, and management, I support each step of the process to drive evidence-based decision-making and deliver actionable insights.

I specialize in data extraction, transformation, and visualization, enabling businesses to optimize processes, forecast trends, and communicate insights effectively. I also offer training in Power BI and best practices in data visualization to help teams build compelling, data-driven reports and dashboards.

I am available for short-term projects as well as part-time, long-term engagements.
